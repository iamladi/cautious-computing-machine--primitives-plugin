## What was borrowed

- Four-principle structure (Think Before Coding, Simplicity First, Surgical
  Changes, Goal-Driven Execution).
- The `❌ Wrong / ✅ Right` paired-block convention used in the workspace's
  `EXAMPLES.md` files.

Principle wording is paraphrased into HAL's voice and cross-linked to local
enforcement skills. No upstream prose is reproduced verbatim; structure and
naming are the borrowed elements.

The derived `SKILL.md` carries an in-band attribution line so the artefact
loaded at invocation time is self-attributing even if separated from this
file.
